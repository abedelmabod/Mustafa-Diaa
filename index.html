Teaching Methods

Interactive Learning: We engage students in class through group activities and discussions, allowing them to apply concepts in practical contexts. We believe that interacting with the material in a hands-on way enhances deep understanding and helps students retain information. Real-World Case Studies: We provide students with real accounting case studies, enabling them to understand how to apply theory in practice. This approach helps students develop analytical thinking skills and solve accounting problems in various work environments. Practical Workshops: We organize regular workshops to enable students to apply what they have learned in simulated real-world environments. Students are encouraged to solve complex problems and work collaboratively, which helps them develop teamwork and critical thinking skills. Blended Learning: We offer a blend of traditional lectures combined with e-learning, allowing students to access educational materials online and review them at their convenience. This method provides students with greater flexibility in their learning and allows them to review lessons according to their personal needs. Continuous Assessment: We rely on continuous assessments through quizzes, practical projects, and class discussions. This ensures that students stay engaged with the material and allows us to track their progress accurately. Personalized Guidance: We ensure individual support for each student through advisory and mentoring sessions, helping students overcome academic challenges and develop their accounting skills effectively. Through these methods, we aim to provide accounting education that focuses on deep understanding and practical application, helping students succeed in the field of accounting and achieve their professional goals.
